About NRJI.

NRJI is a first-of-its-kind performance category: Energy Training. More than a workout, it is an immersive workshop for anyone ready to rise and level up in work, relationships and life. Designed to build self-trust by training the capacity to stay clear, calm and powerful under pressure.

Grounded in evidence-led tools from sports psychology and neuroscience, the experience combines movement, breathwork, visualisation and mindset training to transform the inner critic into the inner champion. The workout is the vehicle. Identity change is the destination.

NRJI treats energy as a trainable resource. Not a mood, not a given. People leave energised, mentally sharper and more confident performing under pressure across every area of life.

The founder

02

Sophie Storm Roberts

Founder, NRJI · Adventure Athlete · Keynote Speaker

One question changed everything: what if I train my energy the way athletes train their bodies?

From no athletic background, Sophie Storm Roberts became a world-first endurance athlete by learning to stay present, courageous and curious under extreme pressure. She became the only person to climb the highest mountains in all eight Alpine countries, and cycle between them, in 32 days.

A global keynote speaker, she has spoken at TEDx and Harvard. She founded L2P24, helping 1,000+ people cycle London to Paris in 24 hours, and TrailBlazers, building confidence and courage in teenage girls. She coaches founders, leaders and athletes to unlock greatness.

NRJI is the culmination of two decades spent proving that extraordinary performance is not reserved for the elite. It can be trained.

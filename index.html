The founder

Trained, not born.

From no athletic background, Sophie Storm Roberts became a world-first endurance athlete by learning to stay present, courageous and curious under extreme pressure.

She became the only person to climb the highest mountains in all eight Alpine countries, and cycle between them, in 32 days. A global keynote speaker at TEDx and Harvard.

Founder of L2P24, helping 1,000+ people cycle London to Paris in 24 hours, and TrailBlazers, building confidence and courage in teenage girls. Coach to founders, leaders and athletes.

NRJI is the culmination of two decades proving extraordinary performance is not reserved for the elite. It can be trained.
